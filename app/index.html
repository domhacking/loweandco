The Lowe family has been acquiring property within prime central London since the 1970s and the company has grown through this experience and the invaluable contacts gained over this period of time.

Etablished by Philip Lowe following the acquisition of a property in 2009, Lowe & Co. is made up of a highly experienced team of property specific; investors, financiers/brokers, solicitors, accountants, architects, surveyors, engineers, interior designers, project managers and contractors.

By means of our robust and extremely experienced team of property professionals, we pride ourselves on our speed and efficiency to facilitate extremely fast acquisitions and sales in order to get deals done.

With our extremely experienced contractors, we look to pass value on to our clients by acquiring quality building materials and skilled labour at the lowest possible prices without compromising on the quality or attention to detail required to meet the rigorous standards that both we and our clients demand of our finished properties prior to sale or letting.

Lowe & Co. sources both residential and commercial property deals that may include some or all of the following characteristics:

- scope to increase internal square footage (with or without planning permission already in place)

- title splits

- lease extensions and/or the acquisition of freeholds

- improved floorplan though reconfiguration and/or

- major cosmetic refurbishment featuring truly exquisite interior design and art work of the highest quality from around the world

Lowe & Co. is currently sourcing property in, but not limited to prime central London, including; Mayfair(W1), Belgravia(SW1), Chelsea(SW3, SW10), Kensington(W8), South Kensington(SW7) and Earls Court(SW5).
