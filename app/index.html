The Lowe family has been acquiring property within prime central London since the 1970s and the company has grown through this experience and the invaluable contacts gained over this period of time.

Established by Philip Lowe following the acquisition of a property in 2009, Lowe & Co. is made up of a highly experienced network of property professionals that help source optimal properties given certain perameters.

By means of our robust and extremely experienced team of property professionals, we pride ourselves on our speed and efficiency to facilitate extremely fast acquisitions.

Lowe & Co. sources both residential and commercial property deals that may include some or all of the following characteristics for our clients:

  • Scope to increase internal square footage (with or without planning permission already in place)
  • Title splits
  • Lease extensions and/or the acquisition of freeholds
  • Improved floorplan through reconfiguration
  • Major cosmetic refurbishment featuring truly exquisite interior design and art work of the highest quality from around the world

Lowe & Co. is currently sourcing property in, but not limited to prime central London, including; Mayfair(W1), Belgravia(SW1), Chelsea(SW3, SW10), Kensington(W8), South Kensington(SW7) and Earls Court(SW5).
